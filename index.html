Our Story

Born From
The Hobby

In September 2021, Troll Aus Pty Ltd was born out of a shared passion for all things Pokémon. As collectors ourselves, we understood the desire for high-quality, affordable Pokémon TCG products in Australia.

This drive led us to become the exclusive Australian partner of Troll and Toad, allowing us to offer competitive prices and unique opportunities to our customers.

We're not just a business — we're fellow collectors who know the excitement of pulling a rare card or completing a set.
